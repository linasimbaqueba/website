About Me

I am a Mathematics graduate student at the University of Victoria, specializing in combinatorics. My research focuses on pattern avoidance, skew Dyck paths, and Sidorenko-type inequalities.

I received my B.S. in Mathematics from Universidad Nacional de Colombia in 2022 and am currently pursuing my M.S. at the University of Victoria. I also work as a Teaching Assistant while continuing my research.

Enjoying the outdoors when not working on mathematical problems

Research Interests

  • Combinatorial Pattern Theory

    Focusing on consecutive pattern-avoidance in Catalan words.

  • Skew Dyck Paths

    Exploring equivalence classes and counting prefixes of skew Dyck paths.

  • Graph Theory

    Working on Sidorenko-type inequalities for pairs of trees.
