About Me

I am an Associate Professor of Mathematics at University of Mathematics, specializing in algebraic topology and geometric analysis. My research explores the intersection of pure mathematics with applications in data science and machine learning.

I received my Ph.D. from Stanford University in 2015 and have since published over 20 papers in leading mathematical journals. I currently lead the Geometric Analysis Research Group and teach graduate-level courses in advanced topology and differential geometry.

Research Interests

  • Algebraic Topology

    Focusing on persistent homology and applications to data analysis.

  • Geometric Analysis

    Working on problems related to curvature flows and minimal surfaces.

  • Mathematical Physics

    Exploring connections between gauge theory and low-dimensional topology.
